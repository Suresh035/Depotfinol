We Are The Best

Depotfinol Pvt Ltd is a dedicated warehouse and logistics company with strong roots in Montenegro and Albania. Our core mission is to simplify the storage and supply chain process for businesses of all sizes.
With a team of skilled professionals and modern infrastructure, we provide tailored solutions for storage, distribution, and inventory management. We believe in safety, speed, and service all delivered with professionalism and efficiency.
Our goal is to become a leading warehousing provider in the Balkan region by combining operational excellence with customer-focused service.

Our Team

Meet Our Team Members

Mr. Viktor Androvic
Founder & Chairman

Depotfinol Pvt Ltd was established in 1990 by Mr. Viktor Androvic, a logistics expert from Central Europe. With a strong background in industrial storage and regional trade, he foresaw the growing need for organized warehouse systems in Southeast Europe.

Mr. Leon Markovic
Chief Executive Officer

Mr. Leon Markovic joined Depotfinol Pvt Ltd in 2000, bringing with him a strong background in supply chain operations and workforce management. Over the last two decades, he has played a central role in transforming the company into a modern, tech-driven logistics brand.

Mr. Stefan Kovac
Project Head

Mr. Stefan Kovac joined the Depotfinol team in 2005 and has since become the backbone of the company’s infrastructure growth. With expertise in engineering, logistics layout planning, and project execution, he manages every step of facility development — from site selection to layout design, construction, and system setup.
